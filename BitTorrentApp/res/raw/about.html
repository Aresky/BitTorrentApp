BitDownload is a free BT file download and sharing client for Android devices.

It will let you search and download files from the BitTorrent network and it will let you share files with other BitDownlod users on the same local network (via WiFi)

BitDownload also features a simple music player that we'll be evolving with more features such as play to TV, play the downloading files and so on.
